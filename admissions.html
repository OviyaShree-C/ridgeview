ADMISSIONS

Our admissions process is designed to welcome students into a supportive and inspiring learning environment where they can grow academically and personally. We encourage families to explore our programs, meet our dedicated educators, and discover the opportunities available to every student. Through a transparent and friendly admission process, we aim to make every new student feel confident and excited about joining our school community.

Open House Information

Our Open House events provide families with the opportunity to experience our school community in a welcoming and engaging environment. Visitors can explore our classrooms, learning spaces, and facilities while gaining a better understanding of the academic programs and opportunities we offer.

During the visit, parents and prospective students can meet our dedicated teachers and staff, learn about our teaching approach, and discover the wide range of activities that support student development both inside and outside the classroom.

Open House is also a great opportunity to ask questions, connect with our school community, and see firsthand how we nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every student.
